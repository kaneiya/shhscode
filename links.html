Sunny Hills Coding Club

In Coding Club, members will work together on projects, get help on their own projects, learn new languages and concepts, and get to know people with similar interests. In addition to teaching programming concepts, we want to involve the members in choosing the concepts being taught, and in teaching each other. Things that we'll do together include game development, web development, app development, and anything else you might be interested in! We also hope to participate in hackathons and coding competitions, possibly traveling for some. If this sounds interesting to you, press the button below to join our Discord server!
